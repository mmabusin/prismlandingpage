FAQ

Questions, answered.

Everything you need to know before you get started.

Do I need a football background to use The Prism?
+
Not at all. Just ask your question in plain English — Prism handles the analysis. Whether you're a professional scout or a weekend fan, the platform works the same way for everyone.
What leagues and competitions are covered?
+
We cover the Premier League, Bundesliga, Serie A, Ligue 1, and La Liga — plus live World Cup 2026 data, updated as matches are played. More leagues are coming soon.
What can I actually ask it?
+
Anything football. "Who are the top young CBs in the Bundesliga?" "How does Salah compare to Mbappé this season?" "Build me a national team shortlist for Germany." "Who are the standout performers at World Cup 2026?" Just type it.
How is this different from other stats platforms?
+
You ask questions, you get answers — not raw data tables to figure out yourself. Every output is structured, contextualised, and designed to help you make a decision. No dashboards to learn, no exports to wrangle.
How current is the data?
+
Live. Data is pulled in real time when you ask. World Cup 2026 match data updates as games are played — so what you get back reflects what actually happened, not yesterday's cache.
Is early access free?
+
Early access is invite-only and free for beta users. We're bringing people in gradually to make sure the experience is right before opening up more broadly. Join the waitlist and we'll be in touch.
